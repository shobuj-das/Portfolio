# From Manual Regression to Risk-Based Automation Coverage

Manual regression alone does not scale. Risk-based coverage helps automate what matters most.

## How I Prioritize
- Business critical user journeys
- Revenue impacting flows
- Areas with frequent production defects

## Execution Model
1. Define critical journeys with product and engineering.
2. Assign risk scores to each journey.
3. Automate top risks first and review monthly.

## Outcome
Teams spend less time on low-value checks and more time preventing expensive defects early.
